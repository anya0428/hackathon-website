We love inspiring!

Helix Hacks is a hackathon designed to inspire and empower young coders to pursue and develop their programming skills. Over the course of 30 hours, teams up to 4 participants will work to develop a software project that helps to solve a real-world problem.

Due to quarantine, we have converted to an online hackathon so that our participants still have the same opportunity to learn and explore. We guarantee the same amount of fun, experience, and opportunity despite the hackathon being virtual.

Our Benefits
  • Zenith Tutoring Presentation: Zenith Tutoring will hold a 45 minute presentation during the hackathon informing students about how to build a computer science career. There will be a Q&A section as well.

  • FLEO Presentation: FLEO will hold a workshop in which they will provide information about basic topics with regards to finance, like investment, money management, interest, and future planning.

  • Internship at Sashido: Get a chance to get an internship at Sashido. More detail and guidance will be provided at hackathon.

  • Many Amazing prizes: We will be distributing Airpods, Go Pros, Polaroids, Portable Mini Printers, NVIDIA's Developer Kits and so much more.

THEME: COVID-19 Pandemic
As everyone knows, one of the most popular global issues currectly is the COVID-19 pandemic. For this reason, we have themed our online hackathon on the global crisis. You can create anything that would relate to and help the pandemic. Some ideas are listed below. *Note: it is NOT mandatory to follow our theme but we stongly suggest that you do.

Design a program or application that informs people about the pandemic - increases/decreases on number infected cases, popular cases and locations, shows populated areas at live time.

Design a program or application that helps maintain health during the pandemic - how to stay safe, how to maintain mental health, how to exercise, what to do if infected? There are many more.

Design a program or application that helps provide entertainment during quarantine. A video game, ideas of quarantined activites, a way to connect with friends, a artificial friend to keep company? These are just ideas.

Design a program or application that helps people obtain material they need safely. Infected packages prevention protocol, a delivery system, safe replacement ideas simulator? This is an important issue.

F A Q

F A Q

Feel free to email us if you still have any questions!

What is our goal?+

Our goal is to inspire young programmers to take on the challenges in the world of coding. We hope to give all our participants a memorable and delightful experience so not only are they encouraged to attend more hackathons, but excited to explore the coding world for themselves.

How much does it cost?+

Absolutely NOTHING! It's free!

Who should attend?+

This hackathon is for all genders, grades 8 - 12, only.

What will I need?+

Fully charged laptop, good wifi connection, comfortable and safe place to work, working microphone and speaker on computer. We also reccomend to have all the following applications downloaded prior to the hackathon.

How many people per team and what if I do not have a team yet?+

Up to 4 people per team. If you wish to have a team of more than four, you may do so, but note that only 4 prizes will be given to each team. If you do not have a team, then don't worry, we will find you one. We encourage you to collaborate with your team from your own homes, but if you choose to meet up, we highly advise utilizing masks and remaining distant.

Do I need any programming experience?+

NO! We have well planned interactive workshops to educate you on almost all the topics you may need. If you have a special request for a workshop, please email us 2-3 weeks prior to the hackathon.

What are the prizes?+

They are very exciting and rewarding prizes. Due to quarantine however, there are more limitations, but we will try our best to make them as appealing as possible. We are planning to mail them to the appropriate address.

How will judging Work? +

Due to our wide range of participants in terms of grade and age, we are most likely going to have a beginners judging category and an advanced judging category. There many more additional categories with prizes. We will also being doing a raffle.

How will an online hackathon work?+

It will be very similar to a normal hackathon, with a few exceptions of course. Almost everything will be done via the internet. We still provide all the resources you may require - support, mentoring, guidance, workshops. We have also made this hackathon longer than typical due to the difficulties and challenges working over the internet may cause.
